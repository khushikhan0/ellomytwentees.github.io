Review on Hello, My Twenties!

Page 2

This is a show about a group of college students who seem normal at first glance. However, as the story progresses, more dark secrets are revealed about each other's pasts.

The story opens with a shy girl named Eun-Jae. She's been living country territory for her life, so being in the big city of Seoul is overwhelming for her. She lives in a house called Belle Epoque with 4 other roommates.

At first, her roommates are mean to her, causing her to break down and cry in front them. They realize how hard they've been on her and ease up. After that, they all begin to bond and start to truly get to know each other.
